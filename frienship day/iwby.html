I Wanna Be Yours

I wanna be your morning text
The one that makes you smile before the day begins.
I wanna be your playlist on repeat
The melody that never fades.
I wanna be your umbrella in the storm,
Your hoodie when it’s cold,
Your quiet in the chaos,
Your warmth when the world feels cold.
I wanna be the reason you look forward to tomorrow,
The one who walks beside you, not ahead, not behind.
I wanna be yours —
Not just for a moment, but for every moment after.

So…

Would you let me be yours?
