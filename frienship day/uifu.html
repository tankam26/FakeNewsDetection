Until I Found You

I was drifting through days,
Chasing echoes and fading rays,
Never knowing love could feel this true —
Until I found you.
I’d heard the songs,
Seen the stars,
But none of them ever felt like ours.
Then you smiled, and suddenly I knew —
I’d been waiting for you.
So here I am, heart in hand,
Not just hoping you’ll understand,
But asking —
Would you let me be the one
Who loves you like the sun loves the morning dew?

Because I never knew what love could do…

Until I found you.
